Images to help identify general tree risk

These photos show common signs that a tree may be at risk. Use them as a visual reference. Contact a certified arborist or a local forestry office for an official evaluation.

Dead branches

Root failure

Loose/peeling bark on trunk

Cracks/splits on trunk

Nearby construction

Poor pruning

Poor taper

Codominant stems

Wounds

Decay

Mushrooms under tree

Unusual leaf size/color

Serpentine galleries
